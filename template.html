Deepfake Detection Reading Group

We meet every two weeks to discuss papers on deepfake detection. Each session features either a paper someone found interesting or an invited speaker presenting their own work (invited talks are denoted by below). Sessions are informal, we often dive straight into the paper together, so as a speaker there is little to prepare. If you'd like to attend or present, feel free to join our mailing list.
