Deepfake Detection Reading Group

We meet every two weeks to discuss papers on deepfake detection. Sessions are informal–we often dive straight into the paper together–so little to no preparation is required. Each session features either a paper someone found interesting or an invited speaker presenting their own work (denoted by below). If you'd like to attend or present, join our mailing list (details below).
